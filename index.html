Meet Nozipho Sibisi

Nozipho Sibisi is a dedicated educator who believes that every learner can succeed with the right support, encouragement, and strategy.

Education

Bachelor of Education (Intermediate Phase), specialising in Natural Science and Mathematics, from the University of the Free State.

Experience

Worked at a private school in Pretoria for 3 months (temporary role) and served as an Education Assistant for 8 months.

Currently

Learning Coordinator for a retail company in Kempton Park, supporting training and learning programmes.

Her teaching style is calm, structured, and learner-focused breaking down concepts into simple steps and helping learners develop strong foundations. Whether your child needs extra support or wants to level up, Nozipho creates a safe space to ask questions, practise, and improve.
